Harry the Hedgehog

Inspired by the Biblical Parable of The Good Samaritan

Teach kids the meaning of true kindness through Harry the Hedgehog! Join Harry on a delightful adventure in a whimsical forest where a proud peacock and a busy lion pass by someone in need. But Harry — a small yet mighty hedgehog — stops to help, showing what it truly means to love your neighbour. Told in joyful limericks with adorable animal illustrations, this retelling of The Good Samaritan brings compassion to life for children aged 2–8.
